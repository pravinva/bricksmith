Databricks reference architecture style.

Brand palette (use exact hex):
- Databricks Navy: #1B3139 (titles, structure, boundaries)
- Databricks Cyan: #00A8E1 (flows, accents, highlights)
- Databricks Lava: #FF3621 (primary emphasis and key platform elements)
- White: #FFFFFF (background)
- Neutral gray: #C4CCD6 (secondary borders/background fills only)

Visual requirements:
- Clean flat 2D architecture style; no 3D, no photorealism.
- White background with subtle grouped containers.
- Strong visual hierarchy with consistent spacing and alignment.
- Left-to-right flow with clear directional arrows.
- Keep labels concise and presentation-readable.
- Do NOT use colors outside the specified palette except grayscale neutrals.
- Do NOT add numbered circles, step bubbles, or numeric callouts unless explicitly requested.

Typography:
- Modern sans-serif (DM Sans / Inter / Helvetica style).
- Crisp readable labels with high contrast.
- Avoid tiny or dense text blocks.

Critical rendering constraints:
- Do NOT render any instruction text in the final diagram.
- Do NOT render style guide phrases, color names, or hex codes as labels.
- Never display words like "Databricks Navy", "Databricks Cyan", "Databricks Lava", or any "#RRGGBB" value in the diagram.
